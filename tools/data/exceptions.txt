0005176 TBM Telly Tennis
        'Telly Tennis.tzx' contains two programs: 'Tennis +3' and 'Tennis 16'.
        The first is the original program from the +3 manual, and the second is
        a 16K-compatible modified version with the PLAY commands replaced by
        BEEP commands.
